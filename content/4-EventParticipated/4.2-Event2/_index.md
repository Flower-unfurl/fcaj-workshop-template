# Reflection on "FCAJ Community Day - Event 2"

### Purpose of the Event
- The event was organized to share hands-on technical knowledge, career orientation, and explore modern learning tools and technologies within the AWS and AI ecosystem.

### Highlights & Main Concepts

#### 1. Hands-on Tool Floci & 3D Simulation Game for AWS Application Flows
- **Speaker:** Huynh Thai Linh
- Introduced the Floci practical support tool combined with 3D interactive simulation models (such as AWS Cloud Quest), visually demonstrating application architecture flows and AWS service interactions.
- Participants experienced deploying cloud infrastructure, configuring networks, and managing services through vivid 3D gamification challenges, turning complex theoretical concepts into intuitive hands-on tasks.

#### 2. Hackathon Experience: AI Voice Verification System Project
- **Speakers:** Huynh Anh Khuong, Minh Quan, Mai Quoc Anh (Full English Presentation)
- Attended an inspiring sharing session presented by 3 speakers fully in English, detailing their Hackathon journey with the "AI Voice Verification System" project.
- The speakers analyzed audio signal processing flows, biometric voice feature extraction, AI verification models, and effective team coordination to deliver a working product under tight competition time constraints.

#### 3. Building Confidence in Work and Study
- **Speaker:** Nguyen Quynh Nhu
- Emphasized the importance of building self-confidence based on core competence and a mindset that embraces learning from mistakes.
- Confidence empowers developers and students to proactively adopt new technologies, present technical ideas in foreign languages, and communicate effectively in international working environments.

#### 4. Web & App Project on Astrology & Horoscope (Tử Vi)
- Presented a unique real-world project combining traditional algorithms with modern technology: A Web & Mobile App for Horoscope and Astrology (Tử Vi).
- The project illustrated how to digitize chart calculation rules, store and process user data on the AWS Cloud, and deliver a smooth, personalized user experience (UX/UI).

#### 5. Overcoming Procrastination in Work and Study
- Analyzed the root causes of procrastination in IT learning and software project execution.
- Provided actionable strategies: breaking large goals into bite-sized tasks (Chunking), leveraging the 2-minute rule / Pomodoro technique, and establishing personal discipline to maintain consistent momentum.

### Key Takeaways
- Gained insights into visual AWS learning methods via 3D simulation games (Cloud Quest/Floci) and architectural flow modeling from speaker Huynh Thai Linh.
- Learned real-world lessons from the AI Voice Verification System Hackathon project from speakers Huynh Anh Khuong, Minh Quan, and Mai Quoc Anh, spanning technical AI pipelines to professional English presentation skills.
- Developed a mindset for cultivating self-confidence from speaker Nguyen Quynh Nhu, overcoming procrastination, and bringing creative application ideas (such as the Astrology project) to life.

### Practical Application
- Utilize 3D simulation tools and Floci in self-study and AWS lab practice.
- Practice technical English presentation and Hackathon-style teamwork for real-world projects.
- Establish time management habits and task decomposition to eliminate procrastination and boost confidence when taking on new responsibilities.

### Event Experience
- The event provided a dynamic and inspiring atmosphere. The presentations balanced high-level technical depth with practical insights into mindset, career growth, and personal development.
